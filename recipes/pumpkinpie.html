Pumpkin Pie

Description

Pumpkin pie is a delicious dessert dish best served with a dollop of whipped cream on top and on any festive holiday of the winter season, especially thanksgiving! What's better than making your own pumpkin pie, completely fresh made and ready to serve hot to accompany your turkey and stuffing :). Recipe from here.

Ingredients

  • 1 9-inch homemade pie crust, or store-bought, deep dish, unbaked
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 2 large eggs
  • 15 ounce can canned pumpkin or fresh pumpkin puree
  • 12 ounce can evaporated milk

Steps

  1. Make sure to preheat oven to 425 degrees F
  2. In a large bowl beat eggs and pumpkin puree together. In a separate bowl, add the sugar, cinnamon, salt, ginger, and cloves, and mix. Then add to pumpkin mixture.
  3. Gradually stir in the evaporated milk. Carefully pour mixture into unbaked pie shell. Make sure to tap the pie pan on countertop to remove air bubbles.
  4. Bake at 425 degrees F for 15 minutes. Reduce temperature to 350 F; bake for 40 to 50 minutes longer, or until the pie is set. Check for doneness by giving the pie a gentle wiggle. The center may jiggle just a tiny bit. It will continue to set up as it cools.
  5. Cool completely. Serve immediately with whipped cream.
